Our Ventures

Explore the exciting ventures of EasyWorld Co. Op

Learn More

Here we expand on the ventures we are pursuing. Each venture is part of our mission to bring innovation and sustainability to local communities.

Cassava Starch Factory

At EasyWorld Co. Op, our Cassava Starch Factory project aims to revolutionize local and international markets by producing premium, high-quality cassava starch. We are committed to sustainable sourcing, modern processing techniques, and scalable production that meets industrial, food, and pharmaceutical demands. By investing or collaborating with us, you join a movement to empower rural farmers, create jobs, and drive profitable growth in a rising global starch industry. Together, we can transform raw potential into thriving economic value — responsibly and innovatively.

Dairy Goat Farming

Our Dairy Goat Farming initiative is more than just livestock — it's a forward-thinking venture focused on producing high-quality, nutrient-rich goat milk using sustainable, ethical, and scalable practices. At EasyWorld Co. Op, we combine traditional expertise with modern veterinary care and technology to ensure optimal animal welfare and premium dairy output. This venture presents a unique opportunity for investors and partners who believe in health-focused agribusiness, rural economic growth, and building a resilient food system in Africa and beyond.
